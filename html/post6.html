CNC Router

In terms of size, older and industrial-strength models of CNC routers are extremely large and can take up a good amount of space.

However, newer desktop CNC routers are much smaller and can sit right on a large table. While these desktop machines are certainly not as powerful as their industrial-size counterparts, they are ideal for home use and can literally connect with a home computer.

A user can simply use software on their home computer and program the desktop CNC router to perform specific production functions.
